⭐︎What supplies you will be needing⭐︎
For beginners the type of supplies you would need is, pencils erasers, paper, sharpener and a ruler. If your more advanced and want to have realism drawing the type of supplies you would need is colored pencils, blending tools, ink pens, alcohol markers, charcoal and graphite sticks.
